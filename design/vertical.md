# Vertical: Independent Creative Agency (IE)

Closest match in the design-extract vertical library: none (no `creative-agency.md` exists yet). This file scaffolds the conversion non-negotiables and anti-patterns from observation of the current site plus general agency-vertical CRO knowledge, ready to be promoted to `~/.claude/skills/design-extract/references/verticals/creative-agency.md` after a second creative-agency project lands.

## Audience snapshot

- Decision makers at Irish SMEs (founder, marketing lead, brand lead) and not-for-profits commissioning multi-service work (brand + web + digital marketing in one engagement).
- Buying mode is **trust-by-portfolio**, not feature comparison. Visitors scan named case studies, judge by client roster quality, then convert.
- Sales cycle: introductory call + chemistry meeting + scoped proposal. No self-serve, no pricing on site.

## Conversion non-negotiables

- **Single primary CTA destination:** "Let's Chat" pinned in the nav, linking to `/contact/`. Identical CTA repeats in the universal `Let's chat.` footer block on every page.
- **Visible client logo bar** within the first scroll on `/about/` (and ideally home) for instant trust.
- **Named portfolio pieces** with logo treatment on the index, large hero + numbered case sections on each detail.
- **Office address + email + phone** on `/contact/` and in every footer (Ireland Companies Act sensitivity for service businesses + local SEO).
- **No pricing**, **no "book a discovery call" calendar embed**: the contact form is the only conversion surface.

## Anti-patterns for this vertical

- Auto-rotating testimonial carousels (eat dwell time on the proof block without giving the visitor control).
- Modal pop-ups gating contact details.
- Pricing teasers ("from $X") on an agency selling £20k+ retainers — devalues the engagement.
- Generic stock photography in the case-study collage (visitors expect the studio's actual output).
- Multi-step contact forms. The current `/contact/` form is single-screen with five fields. Keep it.

## Composition signature observed

- Universal dark navy background (`#0D2030`) on every page.
- Coral handwritten "section signatures" (e.g. "Our Work", "Team", "Get in touch!") in Starlit Drive precede each major block, replacing a conventional eyebrow.
- Butler serif headlines, often very large (`Let's chat.` at ~9rem on desktop).
- Numbered case-study sections (`01`, `02` …) in Butler with a short mint underline.
- Photography on portfolio detail is colour and full-bleed; team portraits on `/about/` are black-and-white in a strict grid (intentional B&W treatment, never desaturate elsewhere per Connor's standing rule).

## Notes for future verticals/service pages

When new vertical or service landing pages are added later:
- Reuse the section-signature pattern (Starlit Drive script in coral or mint).
- Reuse the numbered case section for any "how we do it" block.
- The CTA destination remains `/contact/`; do not introduce a second conversion form on a service page.
- Logos in the trust bar must stay monochrome / low contrast — never re-saturate them to colour.
