We won Gold at the Spider Awards. A very proud moment for Rooftop Twenty Two, and one we shared with the clients who made the work possible.

## Shortlisted across two categories

Going into the night, we had been shortlisted for two projects we are genuinely proud of.

**Best Website, SME** for Kinore, the online accountant. An incredible digital-first business that wanted to look modern and clear without overcomplicating things, and a project we worked on closely with the Kinore team to bring to life.

**Digital for Good** for the Breast Cancer Ireland KNOW MORE campaign. This one was about impact: making vital information clearer, more accessible, and easier to find on a subject where clarity genuinely matters.

![Shortlisted across two categories at the Spider Awards 2026.](/images/latest/spider-awards-2026-gold/inline-shortlist.jpg)

## Home with Gold

On the night, we were honoured to take Gold for our work with Breast Cancer Ireland. Great to have some of the Rooftop Twenty Two team there to celebrate, with a big shout out to those who could not make it.

And of course, thank you to Breast Cancer Ireland and to Eventmaster for trusting us with the work. We are very proud of what we achieved together.

## Why it matters

Awards are never the point of the work, but recognition like this is a useful external check. It tells us the thinking and the craft hold up against the best in the country, and it puts a spotlight on projects, like KNOW MORE, where the message reaching more people is the real win.
