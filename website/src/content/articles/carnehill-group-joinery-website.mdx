We are thrilled to reveal the website we designed for the Carnehill Group, quality joinery and contractors.

We produced a custom website that showcases their exceptional workmanship and commitment to quality. From the clean design to the smooth user experience, every detail was built with precision, just like their projects.

## Let the work do the talking

For a joinery and contracting business, the proof is in the finished projects. The website's main job is to put that craftsmanship front and centre and make it easy for prospective clients to see the standard of work before they ever make contact.

So we built around the work. Clean, uncluttered layouts that give each project room to land, and photography presented in a way that does justice to the detail and finish.

## Precision, end to end

We took the same care with the build that Carnehill take with their joinery. A clean design, a smooth user experience, and attention to the details that are easy to skip and obvious when they are missing. Fast pages, clear navigation, and a structure that guides visitors from admiring the work to getting in touch.

The result is a custom website that reflects the quality of the business behind it, and gives Carnehill a digital presence built to the same standard as everything they make.
