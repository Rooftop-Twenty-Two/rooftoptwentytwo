We are proud to present our work for professional law researchers Ellis & Ellis. Across this project, our team brought together brand identity, brand guidelines, web design, web development and search engine optimisation.

## One team, the full stack

Professional services brands live and die on credibility. For specialist law researchers, the digital presence has to signal rigour and trust before a prospective client picks up the phone. That is hard to achieve when brand, website and search are handled by three different suppliers who never speak to each other.

We handled all of it under one roof. A coherent brand identity, the guidelines to keep it consistent, a website built to present the expertise clearly, and the SEO foundation to make sure the right people find it. Each discipline informed the next.

## Why the order matters for professional services

Identity first, then build, then search. The brand sets the tone and the standards. The website expresses them. SEO makes sure the work is discoverable for the specific, high-intent searches that matter in a niche field like legal research, where volume is low but the value of each enquiry is high.

The outcome is a brand and a website that look the part and a search foundation built to bring in the right kind of enquiry. For a specialist firm, that combination of credibility and findability is exactly what moves the needle.
