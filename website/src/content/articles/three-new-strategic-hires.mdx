Some good news on the eve of the long weekend. Rooftop Twenty Two continues to grow, with three new strategic hires joining the team.

## Niamh Keady, Senior Account Manager

Niamh comes on board as Senior Account Manager, bringing strong strategic thinking and client insight, shaped by her experience working in agency roles in New York.

## Aidan Murphy, Production Manager

Aidan joins as Production Manager, also bringing valuable experience from the US back home to Ireland. He will work closely with our team and our clients to make sure every project is delivered to brief, on time, and to the highest standard.

## Emily McCarthy, Account Executive

Emily steps in as Account Executive, supporting our account management team with a focus on social media and account planning.

## A step forward

Three strategic hires is a meaningful investment in the quality of service we offer as our clients' needs evolve. It is a great step forward for the business, and we are excited for what is ahead.
