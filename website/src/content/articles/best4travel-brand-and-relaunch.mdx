Some weeks are harder than others. This was not one of them. We spent the week on-site in Greece, capturing content in Sani and Ikos for the upcoming relaunch of Best4Travel: a new brand, website, and look and feel we could not be more excited to share.

## Why we shoot on location

For a travel brand, the imagery is not decoration, it is the product. People book a holiday on the strength of how a place makes them feel, and stock photography rarely carries that feeling. Being on location in Sani and Ikos meant we could capture the real texture of the destinations, the light, the detail, the moments that make someone want to be there.

That content becomes the raw material for everything that follows: the website, the brand look and feel, and the campaigns that will drive bookings once the relaunch goes live.

## A relaunch, not a refresh

This is a full relaunch. A new brand, a new website, and a new visual identity, all designed to reflect where Best4Travel is heading rather than where it has been. Capturing the content first, before the build, means the brand and the site are designed around real imagery rather than the other way around.

It is the kind of joined-up project we love: brand, web, and content captured and built together, so the finished article feels cohesive from the first impression to the final booking. More to come as the relaunch lands.
