We are proud to announce that we have been shortlisted for Best Use of SEO at the Digital Media Awards 2025.

Our entry was for HOMS Assist Solicitors, and we could not be more excited to see this project recognised on a stage like this.

## The work behind the nomination

Legal is one of the more competitive corners of search, and the people looking for a solicitor are often at a moment of genuine need. That makes precision matter more than volume. The SEO work for HOMS Assist focused on the searches that actually lead to enquiries, and on building the kind of authority that earns trust in a category where trust is everything.

A special thanks to Talia, our SEO Manager, whose expertise and hard work made it possible, and to Maria Gleason from SoulTribe Agency for her industry insight and collaborative approach.

## A night among good company

It was a privilege to be shortlisted among so many outstanding companies and digital projects, and to celebrate the work at the awards. Being recognised at this level, alongside the best in the business, is exactly the kind of external benchmark we want to be measured against.

It also reflects where the SEO side of the studio is heading. Recognition like this is a sign of the depth we are building in search, and of more to come.
