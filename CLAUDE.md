# Rooftop Twenty Two

Marketing site for Rooftop Twenty Two, a website and digital marketing agency in Dublin. Built with Astro. The app lives in `website/` (run `npm run build` / `npm run dev` from there).

## Content & voice — READ BEFORE WRITING OR EDITING ANY COPY

All site copy (headings, body, CTAs, case studies, emails) must follow the
**[Content Writing Style Brief](docs/content-style-guide.md)**. Read it before
writing or editing user-facing words.

The short version:

- **Write across-the-table.** Plain, warm, honest. If you wouldn't say it out loud to a client, don't write it.
- **Short sentences. Full stops do the work.** Vary the rhythm. A fragment is fine.
- **No em dashes joining clauses.** This is the number-one AI tell. Use a full stop and a short follow-up. One em dash per page max; zero is fine.
- **No emojis. No AI gloss.** Bin "elevate", "unlock", "leverage", "seamless", "robust", "cutting-edge", "passionate", "tailored solutions", "in today's fast-paced digital landscape".
- **Irish/British spelling, always.** optimise, recognise, programme, colour, behaviour, organise. Never -ize/-or/color/program.
- **Results, not vanity.** Real numbers beat adjectives. Sell the outcome, never "hours".
- **Sentence case** for headlines and buttons. CTAs sound like a person asking ("Tell us what you're working on"), never "Submit"/"Learn more".
- **Don't crowbar signatures.** "under one roof" is ours but it's overused. Once per page, max.
- **Binned phrases:** "Don't just take our word for it", exclamation marks (unless genuinely warm).

Run the 10-second check in the brief on every line.
